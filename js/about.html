Our Story

Three generations of baking tradition in Monchique

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1987 by João and Maria Silva, Padaria Branco Quente started as a small neighborhood bakery serving fresh bread to the residents of Monchique.

What began as a modest wood-fired oven operation has grown into a beloved local institution, now run by their daughter Ana and her husband Carlos, who continue the family traditions while adding their own innovations.

Open daily since 1987
